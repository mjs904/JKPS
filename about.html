Our story

About JKPS Solutions

A team of engineers and designers who care about shipping good software.

Where we started

JKPS Solutions was founded in 2019 by four engineers who kept running into the same problem: clients were being handed bloated, over-engineered software that didn't actually solve their problem. We started small — taking on a couple of consulting projects on the side — and grew from there.

Today, we're a team of 30+ engineers, designers, and security specialists working with clients across fintech, healthcare, logistics, and SaaS. We're still headquartered in Bangalore, with a remote team spread across India and Southeast Asia.

We don't have a flashy origin story or a famous VC on our cap table. What we do have is a good track record, clients who come back for the next project, and engineers who take pride in their work.

What we believe in

Simplicity over cleverness

The best solution is usually the simpler one. We resist the urge to add complexity unless there's a real reason for it.

Honest communication

We'll tell you when a timeline isn't realistic or when your idea has a flaw. We'd rather have a hard conversation upfront than a failed project later.

Quality as default

We don't have a "fast and dirty" mode. Every project gets the same level of care regardless of size.

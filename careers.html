What it's like to work here

We're a 30-person team, mostly engineers. We don't have a lot of middle management. Most decisions get made by the person closest to the problem. You'll work on real client projects from day one — not internal tools that nobody uses.

We're fully remote-friendly. Half the team works from Bangalore, the rest are across India and Southeast Asia. We do a full team meet once a year and have bi-weekly video calls by project.

Competitive pay

We benchmark against Bangalore market rates at the 75th percentile. No negotiation games.

Remote-friendly

Work from wherever. We care about output, not office hours. Overlap with IST timezone expected.

Learning budget

₹30,000/year per person for courses, books, and conferences.

Health insurance

Full coverage for you and one dependent. No waiting period.
